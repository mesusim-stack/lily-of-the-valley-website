How to Play

About This Game

Lily Bloom Garden is a mini game created as part of the Flower of the Valleys student blog. Catch falling Lily of the Valley blooms to score points, collect bonus leaves for extra points, and dodge the toxic red berries. The game gets faster every 10 points — how high can you score?
