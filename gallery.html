Photography Tips

Light

Overcast mornings — no harsh shadows, soft even light

Lens

100mm macro lens reveals bell detail invisible to the eye

Aperture

f/8–f/11 for sharp flowers with creamy background blur

Background

Dark soil or bark behind the flowers makes white bells pop
